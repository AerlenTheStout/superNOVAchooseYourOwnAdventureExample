Popular Types of Fox

Here are a few common types of foxes:

Red Fox Arctic Fox Fennec Fox Grey Fox

Red Fox

The most widespread fox species, known for its reddish-brown fur.

Arctic Fox

Found in Arctic regions, its fur changes color with the seasons.

Fennec Fox

A small nocturnal fox native to the Sahara Desert, known for its large ears.

Grey Fox

Unique among canids for its ability to climb trees.
