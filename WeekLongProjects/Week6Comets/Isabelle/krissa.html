Krissa

Krissa is a hardworking citizen or the Underground. At the time of the story, her father has recently been elected as mayor. As a citizen of the Underground, she is human and unable to use magic.

Age : 13

Species : Human

Magic type : None

Krissa is very independent, completing projects on her own. She doesnt like to rely on others, making her seem closed-off to most. But, that doesn't stop her from becoming Ara's best friend in the Underground.
