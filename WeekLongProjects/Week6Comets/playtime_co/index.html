About Dalhousie

Dalhousie University, located in Halifax, Nova Scotia, is one of Canada's oldest and most prestigious universities. Founded in 1818, it offers a rich academic experience in an inclusive and welcoming environment. With world-class research, vibrant student life, and stunning Atlantic Canadian surroundings, Dalhousie continues to foster learning, innovation, and global impact.

  • Bachelor of Engineering
  • Doctor of Medicine (MD)

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
